Hector Cave House

Hector Cave House, carved into the unique caldera cliff for more than 250 years, was originally used as a wine cellar.Then It became a family owned house, which then opened its doors to share its unique character to travelers from around the world.

Location

Giannis’s home is located in Oia, South Aegean, Greece. Hector Cave house is situated in a very convenient location, right on the main path of Oia traditional settlement. Within walking distance, visitors can reach all the highlights of Oia and wonder around picturesque shops, cozy restaurants and romantic caldera-view cafes. Also, the house lies a few steps from the old castle and the best viewing spot for the famous Oia sunset. Although close to local activity, the house provides peacefulness and privacy, allowing guests to relax and enjoy the marvelous view from the terrace.
